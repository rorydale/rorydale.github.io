The Early Glam Show

DAY · TIME

We’re rewinding to 1974—when platform boots met leather jackets in the smoky backrooms of NYC, London, Toronto, and Melbourne. No radio hits—just under-the-radar riffs that lit the fuse for punk, metal, and every subversive genre since.
